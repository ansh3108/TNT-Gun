# TNT Gun Mod
It is a Fabric based mod which allows you to shoot tnt from a custom gun, and also a custom item 'nitro candy', which you can eat to get Speed and you get launched in the air and also a 'gravity anchor' that sucks all the items near you when you hold it in your off-hand.

# Features

## TNT Gun
Forget placing blocks and running away. The TNT Launcher is a high-velocity projectile weapon that fires Custom TNT Entities.
Impact-Ready: Projectiles travel further and faster than vanilla TNT.
Strategic Combat: Clear rooms or landscapes from a safe distance.

## Nitro Candy
It makes you a living robot.
The Surge: Gain Speed V and leave a trail of fire particles.
Kinetic Launch: Slam into any solid wall to trigger a controlled blast at your feet.
Zero-G Safety: You are granted Level 255 Resistance and constant Fall Distance Reset, fly high and land with no damage.

## Gravity Anchor
The ultimate tool for the demolitionist. 
Passive Vacuum: Hold in your off-hand to pull all nearby dropped items toward you from a 10-block radius.
Temporal Freeze: Right-click to freeze all nearby items in mid-air for 5 seconds, allowing for precise looting.
